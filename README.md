# 🏥 MediCare

MediCare is a modern Clinic & Hospital Management Platform that helps clinics manage appointments, patients, doctors, staff, prescriptions, billing, and reception operations from a single dashboard.

---

## ✨ Features

### 👤 Patient Portal
- Patient Registration & Login
- Clinic & Doctor Search
- Book Appointments
- View Appointment History
- Download Prescriptions
- View Medical Records
- Health Tips & Facts Dashboard

### 🏥 Clinic Admin Portal
- Clinic Dashboard
- Appointment Management
- Team Management
- Patient Management
- Billing & Invoices
- Reports & Analytics
- Clinic Profile Management

### 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Portal
- Doctor Dashboard
- My Appointments
- My Patients
- Consultation Management
- Prescription Builder
- Medical Records Access
- Follow-Up Tracking

### 🖥️ Receptionist Portal
- Appointment Approval & Rejection
- Walk-In Patient Registration
- Token Management
- Patient Check-In
- Billing & Payments
- Queue Management

---

## 🎟️ Token Management

Each doctor has an independent token queue.

Example:

Doctor A:
1, 2, 3, 4, 5

Doctor B:
1, 2, 3, 4, 5

Features:
- Generate Token
- Next Token
- Previous Token
- Recall Token
- Skip Token
- Live Queue Tracking

## 💊 Prescription System

Doctors can:

- View Patient History
- Add Symptoms
- Add Diagnosis
- Create Prescriptions

- Generate PDF Prescriptions

Prescription includes:

- Clinic Information
- Doctor Information
- Patient Information
- Medicines
- Dosage
- Frequency
- Duration
- Advice
- Follow-Up Date
